Draw a horizontal line.

Usage:
  hr [-h] [-o OPENING] [-r REPEAT] [-w WIDTH] [-c CLOSING] [-e ENDING]
  hr --help
  hr --version

Options:
  --help      Show this help.
  --version   Show the program version.
  -c CLOSING  The closing character(s) of the line. [default: *]
  -e ENDING   The line ending character(s). [default: \n]
  -o OPENING  The opening character(s) of the line. [default: *]
  -r REPEAT   The character(s) to repeat as the body of the line. [default: -]
  -w WIDTH    The total width of the line, excluding the ending character(s).

Examples:
  [prompt] hr -o '/** ' -r '-+-' -w '22' -c ' */' -e '$'
  /** -+--+--+--+--+- */$[prompt]
